My Work

My work started back in 2006 in Pomona when I tried to get something for my wife but had no money. I decided to get inventive and try something different that could not be purchased in a store but something that was unique and directly for her and also directly made by me. My work began to expand once I was laid off from my IT job and needed to supplement my income for my family. I started selling my designs and built a website hoping my designs would catch on and I took them to the swap meets to have them sold on consignment and worked directly with people on their design elements. Now that I am working again full-time, I do not have as much time to do my artwork but love to use it as a stress reliever and also to help others and to raise money to provide materials for the homeless.
