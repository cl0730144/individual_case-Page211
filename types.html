Types of Art Styles

Abstract - usually about shape, line, value, color, and/or texture.

Art Nouveau - illusionistic but primarily flat, that are highly pattern driven, and that usually incorporate fluid, curving lines.

Manga - Japanese comic book style developed in 19th Century Japan.

Post-Impressionalism - drawings based on light (the preoccupation of the impressionists) but with more attention to geometric shapes.

Realism - convincing representations of reality.

Surrealism - dreamlike and sometimes startling works based on pure imagination.
